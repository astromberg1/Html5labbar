My favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is bicycling on the road with a Racer. I have always liked training and bicycling with a fast racer is the optimal way of training. And there also a lot of gadget around the sport, which I also really like. For a couple of years come the Powermeters thar you can connect to your bicycle-computer. This measure how much power you uses when you bicycle.

I also like that it´s a material sport. You can always be a better cyclist by just bying a better racer. As you probably know Racer it´s name of the a bicycle type for fast bicycling on roads.

What´s the thrill?

The thrill about bicycling is the speed! Sometimes you can go as fast as 50 km/h and if you are on a bike connected 2 millimeter on the ground it really feels. It´s also a challenge to go out and bike 100 km and manage it. After a long ride you feel Good!
